About Our Project

Welcome to Chicken Disease Classifier – an AI-powered web application developed to help poultry farmers and veterinarians identify chicken diseases early using image classification.

🔬 Project Goal

Our goal is to use deep learning and transfer learning to detect and classify common poultry diseases accurately from images. Early detection helps improve poultry health, reduce losses, and assist in informed decision-making.

💡 Key Features

  • Upload chicken images to detect diseases instantly
  • Get detailed disease information and symptoms
  • View research paper links for further reading
  • Fast and easy-to-use interface built with Flask

👨‍💻 Our Team

We are a passionate team of developers and researchers focused on building real-world AI solutions for agriculture and animal health. This project is part of an academic research internship on "Transfer Learning Based Classification of Poultry Disease for Enhanced Health Management."
